ZANKA NO TACHI

"Blade of Ember"

Zanka no Tachi is a fearsome technique in Bleach, wielded by Captain-Commander Yamamoto. It releases intense flames, capable of incinerating anything in its path, symbolizing Yamamoto's unparalleled power and authority.
